What is Tell Me a Story?

Tell Me a Story is the idea of Jack Sanderson-Thwaite, a writer, and Amy Ruffle, a developer.

In bed at the end of the day Amy often found herself wanting Jack to tell her a story. It helped her switch off and go to sleep. Sometimes novels and podcasts just didn't do the job - even the more escapist ones could have exciting, funny and scary sections.

They wondered whether other people had the same problem, and might like to have a collection of stories selected specifically for their calming quality. So they built Tell Me a Story.
